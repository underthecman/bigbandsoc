Bristol University Big Band Society

About Us

We are the University of Bristol's Big Band society, a congregation of the finest, most enthusiastic and friendliest jazz musicians the uni has to offer! Boasting two full bands - our largely unauditioned group BUJO and our fully auditioned group Hornstars - as well as offering smaller ensembeles for more intimate performances, our society is an exciting and welcoming place for players of all abilites.
